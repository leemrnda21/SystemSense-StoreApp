# Restaurant Self-Order System - Staff Login Guide

## Overview
This guide explains how staff members can login to the Restaurant Self-Order Management System.

## Login Methods

### Method 1: Email Address + PIN (Recommended for All Staff)
**Best for:** Managers, Hall Staff, General Login

1. Go to Login Page
2. Enter your **Email Address**: `staff@example.com`
3. Enter your **PIN**: `1234` (your personal 4-digit code)
4. Click "Login"
5. Select your Role (Manager / Hall / Kitchen)

### Method 2: Staff ID + PIN (KDS Devices)
**Best for:** Kitchen Staff using Kitchen Display System (KDS)

1. Go to KDS Login Screen
2. Enter your **Staff ID**: `KIT-001` (printed on your access card)
3. Enter your **PIN**: `1234`
4. System automatically opens KDS dashboard

## First-Time Setup

### For New Staff Members:
1. **Ask your Manager** to create your staff account
2. Your Manager will provide:
   - Your Email Address
   - Your Personal PIN (4 digits)
   - Your Role Assignment
3. Use these credentials to login
4. You'll be directed to role-based dashboard

### For Managers:
1. Go to **Staff Management** section
2. Click **"+ Add Staff"**
3. Fill in staff details
4. Set PIN (must be at least 4 digits)
5. Assign role and permissions
6. Click "Save"
